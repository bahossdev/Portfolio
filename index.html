About Me

I'm a musician and classical guitarist based in Newmarket, Ontario. On the performance front, I take my music across different provinces and countries to connect with diverse audiences. Meanwhile, in the world of research, I've been deeply engaged in a multidisciplinary project. This project involves crafting an app for composing guitar music, where I took charge of designing the algorithm. Collaborating closely with two programmers, we added features, fine-tuned the user interface, and tackled those pesky bugs. This journey not only pushed the boundaries of my musical world but also prompted me to delve deeper into programming. And voila! Here I am, currently immersed as a trainee at this Coding Bootcamp!
